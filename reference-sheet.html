§ 07 — Scripts for the meeting

What to say.

When they say: "I have many projects coming, can you discount?"
"My rate stays the same per project. Once we sign a Preferred Partner Agreement covering 3+ projects with defined scope and an MSA, I unlock a 10% volume discount from the second project onward and priority turnaround. That way the discount is tied to real commitments, not verbal promises."
When they ask: "Are you a registered company?"
"Reframe Studios is a registered MSME sole-proprietorship under the Udyam Registration system. For this engagement scale, that's the optimal structure — payments go to a properly registered entity, and per the MSMED Act you as client get built-in legal protections. Our Udyam certificate and PAN are on the contract."
When they push on price:
"I understand budget is tight. I won't lower the rate — but I can adjust scope. Which features in the Professional tier are not critical to you for Phase 1? We can move them to Phase 2 and bring Phase 1 number down accordingly."
When they ask for source code before signing:
"The demo is hosted on our infrastructure for evaluation. Source code transfers on final milestone payment — standard practice, and in our contract. I can schedule a screen-share walk-through of the code at any stage if you want transparency on progress."
