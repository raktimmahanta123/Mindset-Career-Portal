About Us

A consultancy, not a conveyor belt.

Guwahati deserves a job consultancy that treats both sides of the table with the same respect. We read every CV, interview every candidate, and brief every employer — because a bad match wastes everyone's time. Our office is small on purpose. Our standards are not.

/ 01

For employers

Shortlists you can actually read — three to five candidates, each pre-screened on skill, temperament and context. We handle follow-ups; you handle hiring.

/ 02

For candidates

A one-time registration gets you counselling, resume work, and genuine interviews — not mass-forwarded openings. We back you through the offer stage.

/ 03

For the region

Every placement we make keeps talent in the North-East. We work with estates, hospitality, IT, retail and education across Assam, Meghalaya and Arunachal.
